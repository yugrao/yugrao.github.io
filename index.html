About Me

I'm a second-year Electrical and Computer Engineering Ph.D. student working with Prof. Stephen Boppart at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. With a background in computer engineering and mathematics, I am interested in using computational advances, machine learning, and signal processing throughout varying levels of the imaging process, ranging from optical system design of coherence imaging and nonlinear microscopy to image acquisition to data analysis.
